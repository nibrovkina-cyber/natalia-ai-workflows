# Voice Samples

Drop 5-10 of your actual sent emails here. The skill uses them as few-shot examples to match your writing style.

## Format

Each file should be one email you actually sent. Remove any confidential info (client names, financial details) but keep the tone, sentence structure, and vocabulary.

## What makes good samples

- Routine business emails (not one-off emotional ones)
- Mix of short replies and longer explanations
- Your natural voice, not your "trying to sound professional" voice
- At least 3-4 sentences each

## What happens with these

The skill reads these files before drafting any email reply. It picks up patterns: how you greet people, how long your sentences are, whether you use bullet points or paragraphs, how you sign off.

More samples = fewer edits you make later. 5 is minimum, 10 is ideal.
