# Example email — turning down a project that doesn't fit

Hi,

Thanks for reaching out about the review automation. I looked at your store and want to be honest: with 30 reviews a month, the agent won't save you enough time to justify the setup. You'd spend more hours configuring and tuning it in the first month than you'd save in three months.

What I'd suggest instead: handle reviews manually for now. Set aside 15 minutes in the morning, respond to the negative ones, and move on. When you hit 100+ reviews per month and the manual work starts eating into your actual product time, that's when the agent makes a real difference.

If you want, I can take a quick look at your other processes. Sometimes the biggest time sink isn't the obvious one. I had a seller last month who thought reviews were the problem, turned out invoice processing was costing her 6 hours a week and nobody noticed because "that's just how it is."

Either way, no pressure. Just let me know.

Natalia
