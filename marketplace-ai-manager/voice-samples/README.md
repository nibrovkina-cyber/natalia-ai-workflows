# Voice Samples

Drop 5-10 of your actual past review responses here. The agent uses them as few-shot examples to match your brand tone.

## What to include

- Your real responses to negative reviews (anonymize customer names if needed)
- Mix of different situations: delivery, quality, sizing, packaging
- Your natural voice, not template responses
- Both short (2 sentences) and longer (4-5 sentences) responses

## Why this matters

Without voice samples, the agent writes generic responses. With 5+ samples of YOUR actual responses, approval rate jumps from 50% to 85%+ by day 5.

## Format

One file per response. Name them `response-1.md`, `response-2.md`, etc.
